Quick tips from your Flores Bajawa beans

🌱 Use a medium-fine grind (like table salt) for best crema with your hand press
🔥 Water at 92–96°C (just off boil) for perfect extraction
📏 8 g coffee per shot gives rich, balanced espresso from your Arabica

How to Use Your One Two Cups Mini Hand Press

3-in-1 portable espresso maker (ground coffee + capsules). Follow these steps for perfect shots every time.

1

Prepare your coffee

Grind 8 g of Flores Bajawa Arabica to a fine espresso grind (table salt consistency). Use the included scoop.

💡 Pro tip: For stronger shots, you can go up to 10–12 g if your basket allows (max ~18 g for double)
2

Assemble the portafilter

Unscrew the portafilter from the body. Remove the filter basket. Fill with ground coffee. Lightly tamp (don’t press too hard). Reinsert basket and screw portafilter tightly back onto the body.

3

Add hot water

Fill the water chamber with hot water (92–96°C) up to the marked line (~80–100 ml). Screw the chamber onto the body.

4

Extract the espresso

Place the device over your cup. Unlock the piston (twist if needed). Press/pump the plunger steadily 10–15 times until the shot is complete (~25–40 ml espresso with crema).

☕ You should get rich crema like the photos you shared!
5

Clean & store

Rinse all parts with warm water immediately after use. Dry completely. The device is portable and durable — perfect for daily use at home in Semarang.

Works with ground coffee (recommended) or Nespresso® capsules. For best results with your Arabica beans, always use fresh grind.
