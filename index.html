Processing Methods

🌧️

Wet-Hulled (Giling Basah)

The signature Indonesian method. Parchment is removed at high moisture content (30-40%), then dried.

BodyVery High
AcidityLow
FlavorEarthy, Herbal
💧

Washed

Fermented in water tanks to remove mucilage, then dried with parchment intact. Clean and bright.

BodyMedium
AcidityHigh
FlavorClean, Floral
☀️

Natural / Dry

Dried whole in the cherry. Rare in Indonesia but emerging for fruity profiles.

BodyHeavy
AcidityMedium
FlavorFruity, Sweet
